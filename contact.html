Directions

Getting Here

Stellenbosch is easily accessible from Cape Town and the surrounding region.

directions_car

By Car

From Cape Town, take the N2 towards Somerset West, then the R44 into Stellenbosch. The Stellenbosch University campus is centrally located. Follow signs to the Science Faculty. Visitor parking is available on campus.

train

By Train

The Metrorail Cape Town–Stellenbosch line stops at Stellenbosch station, approximately 1.5 km from campus. Taxis and shuttle services operate between the station and the university.

directions_bus

By Bus

Intercape and other long-distance coach services connect Stellenbosch to major cities. The Stellenbosch University Hoppa shuttle operates within the campus and surrounding areas for students and staff.
