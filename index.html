About the Department

Africa's frontier of computing research

The Department of Computer Science at Stellenbosch University is one of Africa's premier computing environments. We combine deep theoretical foundations with meaningful real-world application — training the engineers, scientists, and thinkers who will define Africa's technological future.

Our research spans artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, bioinformatics, algorithm design, and human-computer interaction. We work closely with industry, government, and global institutions to ensure our scholarship creates lasting societal impact.
